Neon Light

One of the lights that can be created to have attractive colors is neon lights. As the name implies, this lamp contains argon or neon gas which can produce different colors. The gas contained in this lamp will light up when there is an electric current that passes through the gas. When the fluorescent lamp is on, the electric power it needs will be reduced and it will be more energy efficient. Neon lights are widely applied as decorative lights that have attractive colors.

Compact Fluorescent Light

The type of fluorescent lamp that comes with a smaller size is the same as the size of an incandescent lamp. However, compared to incandescent lamps, the power consumption of these lamps is more efficient and does not waste electricity. It can be said that this lamp is one of the most frequently used types of lamps for most home.

Fluorescent Light

Lamps that are considered to replace the presence of incandescent lamps. One of the reasons this lamp is considered as a substitute for incandescent lamps is that it consumes less power, but can produce light that is just as bright. In addition, the resulting light beam feels more comfortable and not too sharp for the eyes.
